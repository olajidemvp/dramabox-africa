# Wahala — 4-Week Validation Growth Plan

Goal: drive enough cold traffic to answer one question — **do people want this and will they pay?**
Scoreboard: https://wahaladrama.vercel.app/insights

---

## Week 0 — Setup (2-3 days)

- [ ] **1. Buy domain** (`wahala.tv` / `watchwahala.com`, ~$10-30). Do this FIRST — links shared before this break later.
- [ ] **2. Compress teaser video** to under 3MB (send Claude a smaller export, or install ffmpeg).
- [ ] **3. Create accounts** named "Wahala": TikTok, Instagram, X, WhatsApp Community.
      Bio: *"Short African dramas. First episodes free. 👉 [link]"*
- [ ] **4. Ask Claude to "build the marketing kit"**: WhatsApp share buttons, invite-for-coins loop, channel-tracked links, 10 trailer scripts, 30 captions.

## Week 1 — Seed (free)

- [ ] **5. Make 5 TikToks.** Formula: series hook as text-on-screen over dramatic footage → "First episodes free — link in bio." 30-60s. CapCut.
- [ ] **6. Post 1/day** on TikTok, repost to IG Reels.
- [ ] **7. WhatsApp blast:** link + best clip to 20-30 groups. Ask 10 friends to forward to 3 groups each.
- [ ] **8. Post the story once:** X thread ("building the African DramaBox, demo live →"), 3 Facebook Nollywood groups, Nairaland TV section.

## Week 2 — Push (free)

- [ ] **9. Keep posting 1-2 clips/day.** Check /insights nightly — note which channel brings visitors.
- [ ] **10. DM 10 micro-influencers/skit-makers** (10k-50k followers): post the trailer + pitch the /creators page. ₦5-20k each max, only if asked.

## Week 3 — Spend small

- [ ] **11. Boost best TikTok with $50** — Nigeria/Kenya/Ghana, 18-34.
- [ ] **12. Kill dead channels, double the winner.**

## Week 4 — Close and decide

- [ ] **13. "Last chance" push:** founding-viewer spots close Friday — bonus coins forever. Post everywhere.
- [ ] **14. Read /insights against targets:**

| Metric | Commit threshold |
|---|---|
| Onboarding completion | > 35% |
| Paywall → pay-click | > 8% |
| Payment leads | > 3% of visitors |
| Founding reserves | 150+ |
| Day-2 return | > 10% |

**Decision rule (set before seeing data):**
- 3+ thresholds hit → commit: license/produce content, real payments (Paystack), launch.
- 2 hit → extend test 2 weeks on the best channel only.
- 0-1 hit → stop; pivot concept, price, or market before spending on content.

---

*Positioning line for everything: "DramaBox collects your money in dollars. Wahala is ours — African stories, African prices, pay with M-Pesa/MoMo/OPay."*
