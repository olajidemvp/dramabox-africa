Wahala — the short drama app for Africa

Binge addictive short dramas made for your phone: vertical episodes of 1–2 minutes, with stories from Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, Uganda, Tanzania, Egypt, Senegal and Rwanda. The first episodes of every series are free. Later episodes unlock with coins you can top up using M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, OPay or your card.

Why Wahala beats long-form streaming in Africa

Short episodes fit short breaks — okada rides, queues, lunch. Data Saver mode keeps episodes light enough for 3G. Prices are in local currency (Naira, Shillings, Cedis, Rand) and top-ups work with the mobile money you already use. No credit card needed.

Frequently asked questions

What is Wahala?

A short drama streaming app made for Africa: 1–2 minute vertical episodes with African stories, languages and settings.

Is Wahala free?

The first episodes of every series are free, and daily check-in rewards give you free coins for more.

How do I pay in Nigeria, Kenya or Ghana?

M-Pesa (Kenya, Tanzania), MTN MoMo (Ghana, Uganda), Airtel Money, OPay and bank transfer (Nigeria), or Visa / Mastercard / Verve.

Does it work on slow internet?

Yes — episodes are small and Data Saver mode cuts video data use for 3G connections.
