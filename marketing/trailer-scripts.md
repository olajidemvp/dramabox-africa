# Wahala — Trailer Scripts (all titles)

Shootable 30-60s vertical trailers. Phone + 1-2 actors + CapCut. Every trailer
ends with the same CTA pattern: cliffhanger freeze → "First episodes FREE —
link in bio." Post to TikTok, repost to IG Reels.

Production notes (all trailers):
- Vertical 9:16, 1080p. Natural light or one ring light.
- Text-on-screen carries the story — assume viewers watch muted first.
- Cut every 2-3 seconds. Zoom-punch on reveals.
- Use trending audio low under VO where allowed.
- End card: Wahala logo red on black + link sticker.
